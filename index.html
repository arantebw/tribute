Dennis MacAlistair Ritchie

The C programming language creator, UNIX OS co-developer, and
"The C Programming Language" book co-author

Timeline

2005

  • The Industrial Research Institute awarded Ritchie its Achievement Award in recognition of his contribution to science and technology, and to society generally, with his development of the Unix operating system.

2011

  • Awarded the Japan Prize for Information and Communications for his work in the development of the Unix operating system.
  • Died at the age of 70.
